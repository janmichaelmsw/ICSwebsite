Insurance

My counseling services may be covered in full or in part by your health insurance. Please check your coverage carefully by asking the following questions, and be alert of policy changes as they occur:

  • Does my health insurance plan include mental/behavioral health benefits?
  • What is my deductible and has it been met?
  • How many sessions per year does my insurance cover?
  • What is the amount per session? Do I have a co-pay (co-insurance), if so, what is my copay amount?
  • Is approval required from my primary care physician?
  • If I have a Medical Savings Account, how do I submit for reimbursement?
