Jan Michael, MSW, LICSW

Therapist and Licensed Clinical Social Worker

Life can thrash us around— I am passionate about helping people grow stronger through it, with increased courage, confidence, calm, clarity, creativity, connection, compassion and curiosity. 

I'm trained in the evidence based Internal Family Systems (IFS) model, and experience it to be honoring, powerful and an effective healing modality for many. Sessions might also incorporate aspects of other therapy models such as Cognitive Behavioral Therapy, boundary education, Brene Brown's work on shame and vulnerability, grief and mourning attention, TED talks, collages and eclectic other ways to support goals. Listening deeply to a client's story informs our unique path.

Parker Palmer writes, "The soul speaks its truth only under quiet, inviting, and trustworthy conditions." I offer a supportive and non-judgmental environment as we work together to be curious about what's not working.

My clients are adults, people of all faiths, and those who don't want to incorporate spirituality in therapy.

Research shows a good connection with your counselor is critical in reaching desired outcomes. I'm glad to offer brief consultations for those who would find this valuable.
